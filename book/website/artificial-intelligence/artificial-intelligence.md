# Guide for Artificial Intelligence

***This guide covers topics related to using and extending artificial intelligence systems in research.***

Artificial intelligence systems are increasingly used to support research workflows, from literature discovery and code generation to documentation, data stewardship and project management.
These systems can be useful, but they also introduce new questions about reproducibility, accountability, security, privacy, authorship and maintenance.

In this guide, we collect guidance for using AI tools in ways that are transparent, reviewable and appropriate for research contexts.
This includes practical standards for documenting how AI agents are extended, how they connect to external services and how research teams can assess the risks of using shared agent configurations.
